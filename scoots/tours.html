Tours

Welcome to Cozumel Island, located in the Caribbean Sea, where the sunshine and happiness are free.

  1. ATV Tours

    "There is no better way to explore the dense jungles and beautiful but deserted beaches of Cozumel's East shore than on an ATV tour. The Eastern half of Cozumel is virtually uninhabited, and seldom visited. There is only one road, and that ends before you reach your destination! You'll board your ATV and ride through the wilds of Cozumel island, visiting Mayan Villages, snorkeling on remote coral reefs, and even seeing ancient Mayan Ruins and caves! These ATV tours are great cruise excursions too - only 2 to 4 hours in length, you can see the island and still have time to explore!" Cozumel-tours.com

  2. Mayan Ruins Tours

    "The Mayan Ruins of the Yucatan Peninsula are truly a treasure to behold. These lost cities hail back to as many as 2,000 years ago, when the Mayas were practicing such mathematical genius as astronomy and physics, long before the white man had figured out the secrets. Nobody knows what happened to the Mayas, but their legends - and their cities - live on to this day. Every visitor to Cozumel should visit Chichen Itza, Tulum, or Coba Mayan ruins at least once on their vacation. This is something truly unique to this region, and the stories that these ruins tell will move you." Cozumel-tours.com

  3. Jeep Tour With Beach, Snorkel And Lunch

    "Discover the very best that Cozumel has to offer as you drive round the whole island on this scenic Jeep adventure. Snorkel a shallow reef, enjoy a beach break and amazing ocean views, see a small Mayan ruin, have an authentic Mexican lunch, look for crocodiles, climb an historic lighthouse. . ." Thisiscozumel.com
