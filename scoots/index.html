Welcome to ~Scoots

We are a Motor Scooter Rental company located on the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexixo in the western Caribbean Sea. We have two rental locations. One is near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel and the other is near the Terminal Puerta Maya further South along the coast. We also offer Jeep and side-by-side ATV rentals.
