Welcome Fishermen!

Fish Haven is located on the West edge of Bear Lake. Bear Lake is known for its beautiful turquoise color and tourist attractiveness. For those people who love the sport of ice fishing, then this is the place for you. Bear Lake freezes over 7 out of every 10 years and its home to trophy cutthroat trout and lake trout.
