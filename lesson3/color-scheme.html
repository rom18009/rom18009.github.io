Background

The background of the website will be #EFF1F3 Cultured White. This sets a nice background that's simple and allows the others colors to really stand out.

Navigation Bar and Footer

The navigation bar will have the background of #FED766, Orange Yellow Crayola. Hovering over a navigation box highlights wit the #009FB7, Blue Merril. The footer is reversed of the navigation bar. The backround for the footer is #696773, Dim Gray with the font of #FED766, Orange Yellow Crayola.

Headers and Body

The Headers will use the color #009FB7, Erie Black for a crisp contrast. Regular text is the regular black color.
