Background

The background of the website will be #EFF1F3 Cultured White. This sets a nice background that's simple and allows the others colors to really stand out.

Navigation Bar and Footer

The navigation bar will have the background of #26313A, Midnight with the font color of #E89700, Gamboge. Hovering over a navigation box highlights will use the #5FB0D9, Malibu. The footer is much like the navigation bar except the main font is #FFF, White with a #E89700, Gamboge on the last updated.

Headers and Body

The Headers will use the color #D85423, Chilean Fire for a crisp contrast. Regular text is the regular black color.

Floating Color

The reamining color of #59A86A, Fern will be used where the site needs a little more contrast or excitment.
