The Happy Farmer

Starting before the sun comes up and going to bed well past the sun going down, life on a farm is hard and involves full days of work. This paints a rough picture and a lot of people would wonder, is it worth being a farmer? Do they ever have joy and happiness? John Olsen has worked his family farm since he could remember. John told me, "It's a hard life and my body is feeling the years of labor, but I wouldn't want anything else." He went on to explain, "All the hours of preparing the soil, planting the seed, watering, and then harvesting brings a sense of accomplishement. I can look back and I know I've done something amazing." You can tell by the large smile on John's face... find out more.
